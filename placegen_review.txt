ICY DWARF LAND
==============

ENVIRONMENT PROFILE
-------------------

Name

- Alpine tundra

Climate summary

- Cold, windy highlands with long winters and short summers

Weather

- Clear cold
- Hard wind

- Heavy snow

Vegetation

AREAS
-----

Natural Area: mountains

- Drunurnar Mountains
- An ice-capped range fills the western Land.

Natural Area: forest

- Krokskogur Forest
- Dwarf pine covers the valley below the mountains.

Natural Area: lake

- Lake Hornindal
- A great cold lake fills the basin. Ice remains in its coves and along its
  northern shore through the short summer.

Settlement Area: capital

- Bjorgheim
- A walled stone city stands where the mountain roads meet.

Settlement Area: northern town

- Roros
- A remote stone town guards the northern pass. Snow lies against its walls
  through most of the year.

Settlement Area: southern fishing and trade town

- Svalaver
- A stone fishing town stands on the southern shore. Dwarf and human
  merchants meet beside its jetty.
